In ancient times, when darkness threatened to consume the world, a radiant hero appeared and valiantly drove back the shadows, heralding an era of peace to the land of Chromatia. As centuries passed, the memories of the terrible darkness faded into legend, and the people of Chromatia became bitterly divided in their struggles to rule the kingdom. The Ivory King has fallen ill, weak from old age, and a new ruler must be chosen to take his place before the ever-encroaching Blacklands engulf the kingdom. Each of the mighty generals from the six realms desires the throne for themselves, thus the drums of war begin to sound. Only you can lead your people to victory and claim the throne for yourself in the chaos to come!

So What Is Stratagem: Chroma Commander?

Stratagem is a vibrant homage to the strategy RPG games of old. We at Sneaky Little Studios wanted to honor the classics, while revitalizing the gameplay with intense multiplayer options that allow you to battle your friends from your smartphone or tablet computer. It is in that spirit that we welcome you to the land of Chromatia where a fierce fight for the throne has begun. You choose one of six commanders as your in-game avatar, ranging from the dull-witted but noble Red Knight to the dark and sultry Purple Witch. To protect your commander, you will form an army from a roster of minions that continually grows as your reputation increases. Battles are fought on a grid map, and you claim victory by defeating your opponent’s commander. But you must choose your actions wisely, as each turn you are granted a limited number of “Action Points” that are used to move and attack with your units. Winning battles grants you wealth and power while strengthening your units for future confrontations.

How Much Will It Cost?

Nothing! Stratagem: Chroma Commander will be free to download and free to play.
